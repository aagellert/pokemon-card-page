Introduction

Pokemon Trading Card Game (TCG) is a competitive online card game in which players will face against the best within their league across the world.

The game consists of decks built with 60 cards: pokemon, items, tools, support cards and stadiums.

Depending upon the format of the game (standard, expandedâ€¦etc) will determine the types of cards allowed and the overall design of the deck to win a match.

In order to win multiple different types of strategies can be utilize to tilt the playing field in your favour.

  • Defense paired with energy drain
  • Offense paired with multiple bench attackers
  • Winning within first round
  • Slow burn energy draining decks

"TCG has been a competitive game since 1996 and is translated into 10 different languages in 74 countries."

Deck Building: For Beginningers

  • Buy theme decks for they are a complete set to get started(free theme decks are provided in game)
  • Research which decks you wish to play before building
  • Download deck list from the Pokemon World Tournament
  • Learn how to use the trade system to get the best cards
