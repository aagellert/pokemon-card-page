Slyveon is a risky deck to play. It is nearly impossible to win the traditional way by winning prize cards. Therefore, the goal to win is by decking-out the other player. This is accomplished by removing all of the opponents: energy, cards in deck and hand. Also, preventing the opponent from protecting their pokemon from Crushing Hammer with Field Blower to remove pokemon tools.
